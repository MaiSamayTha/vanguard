PRIVACY POLICY

Vanguard STEM Academy is committed to collecting, using, storing, and protecting personal information responsibly and in accordance with applicable laws and best practices.

Information We May Collect

Depending on how you interact with Vanguard, we may collect information including names, email addresses, telephone numbers, mailing addresses, educational records, scholarship information, payment information, application materials, device information, browser type, IP address, cookies, and website usage analytics.

How Information Is Used

Information collected may be used to:

  • Process applications and registrations
  • Administer educational programs and services
  • Communicate with students, families, and participants
  • Process tuition and program payments
  • Improve our website, services, and educational offerings
  • Evaluate scholarship eligibility
  • Measure program outcomes and organizational impact
  • Comply with applicable laws
  • Protect the security and integrity of Vanguard's systems

Information Sharing

Vanguard STEM Academy does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only when necessary with trusted service providers, payment processors, educational partners, technology vendors, or governmental agencies where required by law or necessary to provide Vanguard services.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, misuse, or destruction.

Although no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, Vanguard continually works to maintain appropriate security measures.
