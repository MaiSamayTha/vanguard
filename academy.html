Grades 8–12 · Project-Based STEM Academy

The Vanguard Academy

A project-based STEM and workforce academy where students form consulting teams, tackle authentic challenges, and present real recommendations to executives — graduating with experience, not just a certificate.

Overview

Who It Serves & How It Works

The Academy welcomes middle and high school students — particularly under-resourced and emerging student leaders — ready to trade passive learning for real consulting experience. No prior STEM or business background is required, only curiosity and commitment.

  • Grades 8–12, with cohorts grouped by grade band and interest
  • Small consulting teams paired with a faculty lead and executive mentor
  • One capstone challenge per cohort, delivered to a live executive panel

Our students walk in as curious teenagers and walk out as consultants who've briefed real executives.

— The Vanguard Academy Model

Admissions

How to Apply

Admission is rolling until each cohort fills. Applications are reviewed as they arrive.

1

Inquire

Tell us about the student and their interests.

2

Apply

Submit the short online application — no essay required.

3

Interview

A brief conversation with a Vanguard team member.

4

Enroll

Accepted students confirm their seat and join a cohort.

Eligibility

  • Grades 8–12 — no prior STEM or business experience needed
  • Open to students in Atlantic City and beyond, with virtual and in-person cohort options
  • Financial aid and sponsorship available for qualifying students
