Become a Mentor

Vanguard mentors share real expertise with students who are hungry to learn how the working world actually operates — through fireside chats, capstone panels, and one-on-one guidance.

  • Evaluate and coach student capstone presentations
  • Join panels and fireside chats a few times per cohort
  • Optionally provide ongoing 1:1 mentorship to a student
